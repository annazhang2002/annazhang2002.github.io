Project Summary

The goal of my project is to simulate the creatures, sites, and actions of Yosemite National Park. In order to do this, I utilized java inheritance as well as interfaces to simplify my code. I was able to implement the features including, waterfalls, animals, landmarks and other basic numberical facts. In my driver, I have also included bits of user input for trivia, and collecting the fees.

Personal Biography

Anna Zhang is a hardworking junior at Aragon High School who is interested in pursuing a career in computer science. She is a member of the Aragon Robotics Team, future ASB President, and the founder of LEAP Club (an environmental awareness club at Aragon). Anna is determined to make a difference in her community and in the world.
