Anna Zhang is a hardworking junior at Aragon High School who is interested in pursuing a career in computer science. She is a member of the Aragon Robotics Team, future ASB President, and the founder of LEAP Club (an environmental awareness club at Aragon). Anna is determined to make a difference in her community and in the world. At school, she is an active member of the Leadership program and has worked in areas from clubs to publicity to town hall discussions to student council. In terms of helping the community, Anna is currently working on organizing a hackathon for high school students that allows them to code projects that encourages them to help social awareness causes such as world poverty, women's rights, environmental protection, water access, human trafficking etc. Finally, in terms of helping the world, Anna has been passionately bringing attention to environmental issues such as deforestation, global warming and pollution through her Leaders for Environmental Awareness and Protection Club.
