Personal Biography

Anna Zhang is a hardworking freshman at Yale University who is interested in pursuing a career in computer science. Determined to make a difference in her community and in the world, Anna dreams of developing innovative technology that can specifically preserve the environment. After spending several weeks studying abroad in Costa Rica, Anna learned about the importance of environmental conservation, which motivated her to dedicate her life to saving the Earth. She envisions a myriad of potential career paths ranging from software engineering to product management to environmental policy to even possibly founding her own eco-friendly tech company. As a student activist, Anna advocates for a variety of causes such as women's rights, environmental protection, minority representation etc.
