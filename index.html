Anna Zhang is a hardworking freshman at Yale University who is interested in pursuing a career in computer science. Determined to make a difference in her community and in the world, Anna dreams of developing innovative technology that can specifically preserve the environment. After spending several weeks studying abraod in Costa Rica, Anna learned about the importance of environmental conservation, which motivated her to dedicate her life to saving the Earth. Anna Zhang is a hardworking senior at Aragon High School who is interested in pursuing a career in computer science. She is a member of the Aragon Robotics Team, ASB President, and the founder of LEAP Club (an environmental awareness club at Aragon). Anna is determined to make a difference in her community and in the world. At school, she is an active member of the Leadership program and has worked in areas from clubs to publicity to town hall discussions to student council. In terms of helping the community, Anna is organized a hackathon for high school students called Digitize that allows them to code projects that encourages them to help social awareness causes such as world poverty, women's rights, environmental protection, water access, human trafficking etc. Finally, in terms of helping the world, Anna has been passionately bringing attention to environmental issues such as deforestation, global warming and pollution through her Leaders for Environmental Awareness and Protection Club as well as being the environmental rights chair for the San Mateo Activism Coalition (SMAC), a youth activist group. She has planned different events such as walk outs and beach clean ups that encourage the local community to get more involved with changing the political climate.
